Muhammed Ali Mehmood

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Imperial College London, under the supervision of Prof. Ewelina Zatorska.

My research interests are centred around the analysis of PDE systems arising from (compressible) fluid mechanics which model complex flows such as traffic and multi-phase fluids (e.g. the Aw-Rascle model). I am also interested in the use of machine learning to study solutions to such models, particularly using Physics-Informed Neural Networks.

I am also broadly interested in quantitative finance and machine learning. On the projects page or my GitHub, you can find a few small projects that I’ve worked on.
