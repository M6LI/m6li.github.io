I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Imperial College London, under the supervision of Prof. Ewelina Zatorska. My research interests are centred around the analysis of PDE systems arising from (compressible) fluid mechanics which model complex flows such as traffic and multi-phase fluids (e.g. the Aw-Rascle model). I am also interested in the use of machine learning to study solutions to such models, and in particular using Physics-Informed Neural Networks.
